One small step for a bear…

As Senior Product Designer at Pawprint, I furthered their mission to reduce customers’ Scope 3 emissions one small action at a time.

Pawprint’sPawprint’s product approach runs on Objectives and Key Results (OKRs). Here are a few that I worked on.

  • Enable three customers to place their employees into segments
  • 90% of customers who run a scope three survey want to run another
  • Increase the speed of discovery and delivery
  • Increase conversion rate into the business’ engagement group
  • Employees can better understand their organisation’s sustainability journey

AsAs an ardent collaborator, I was deeply involved in listening to customers, collating research, and workshopping problems & solutions with the entire team.

ByBy supercharging the Figma design system and refactoring the front-end React codebase, I significantly reduced the time from discovery to delivery.
