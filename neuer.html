Unearthing the paths to reduced emissions

I’ve been central to evolving NeuerEnergy’s core product from a carbon accounting platform into a smart initiative planning system.

I elevated the existing design language over time to establish a more professional, cohesive visual vocabulary, while at the same time improving the hierarchy and usability of the platform.

WithAs my previous experience in creating and maintaining design systems, I migrated all product design from Adobe XD into Figma, implementing component libraries to optimise the design workflow.

II heavily augmented our design / engineering communication. Through close collaboration with engineers I introduced much more comprehensive detail into all work items.

  • Fully-annotated Figma dev mode views
  • Guides for responsive layouts
  • Guides for both light and dark modes
  • All UI states including hover, disabled, no data, errors, edge cases…
  • Edge case guides for components
  • Sample UI animations
  • Thorough explanation of calculations and behaviours

InFor partnership with our product managers, sustainability consultants, and customers, I’ve contributed multiple new features that enable customers to identify the most cost-effective and high-impact opportunities to reduce emissions throughout their organisations.
